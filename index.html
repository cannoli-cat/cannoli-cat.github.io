About Me

Hi, I'm Tyler Wolfe. I create games and game development tools in Unity with a focus on procedural generation, stylized rendering, and systems that give developers more freedom to create. I'm especially drawn to problems where a little extra engineering can save hours of manual work. My goal is to not only make development faster and easier, but also to empower creators to make more ambitious and unique projects.
